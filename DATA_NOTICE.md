# Data Notice

This repository includes derived residual, labeling, validation, and control artifacts used by the Paper 2 reproducibility packet. It does not redistribute raw SPARC rotmod files or SPARC Table1 metadata.

Primary SPARC citation:

Lelli, F., McGaugh, S. S., & Schombert, J. M. 2016, AJ, 152, 157, "SPARC: Mass Models for 175 Disk Galaxies with Spitzer Photometry and Accurate Rotation Curves", doi:10.3847/0004-6256/152/6/157.

SPARC Zenodo archive:

Lelli, F., McGaugh, S. S., & Schombert, J. M. 2016, "SPARC Galaxy Rotation Curve", Zenodo, doi:10.5281/zenodo.16284118.

The included Paper 1 inputs are derived reproducibility artifacts copied only because the Paper 2 scripts require those exact relative paths. Downstream users should cite the original SPARC paper, the SPARC Zenodo data release, and the Paper 1 reproducibility package when using those derived inputs.
